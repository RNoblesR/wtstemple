Memorial Hall

The Memorial Hall was originally a room in which Taoist priests practiced Taoism in peace and seclusion. It is now the only hall dedicated to Sik Sik Yuen’s priests who are no longer among the living. The Memorial Hall was rebuilt in 1982 and refurbished in 2007 (the year of Dinghai) to add a space to install a platform and lotus seat for deceased priests
